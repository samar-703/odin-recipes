Pavbhaji Recipe

Description

Want a meal that's hearty, satisfying and delightful too? Here's it mashed spiced vegetables; served with a side of cotton-like soft, buttery dinner rolls, crunchy onions, herby fresh coriander and tangy lemon. To sum up, the Pav Bhaji – a famous street food from the ‘city of dreams,’ Mumbai. This Pav Bhaji Recipe is going to be create fireworks on your palate!

Ingrediants

  • 3 potatoes (medium-sized) 250 grams
  • 1 to 1.25 cups chopped cauliflower 120 to 130 grams
  • 1 cup chopped carrot
  • 1 cup green peas fresh or frozen
  • 2.25 to 2.5 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon chopped green chilies

Steps

  1. Chop and boil a mix of potatoes, cauliflower, peas, and carrots until they are soft.
  2. Mash the boiled vegetables using a potato masher or fork until you get a coarse texture.
  3. In a pan, heat oil, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft
  4. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt to the onion-tomato mixture. Mix well.
  5. Add the mashed vegetables to the spice mixture in the pan. Mix thoroughly, ensuring the vegetables are well coated with the spices.
  6. Let the mixture simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  7. Toast pav (bread rolls) with butter. Serve the hot pav bhaji alongside some chopped onions, lemon wedges, and extra butter.
